Kane Ink

Kane Ink is an AI-powered social media automation platform designed to help creators and businesses manage, generate, organize, and publish content across multiple digital channels.

What the application does

Kane Ink provides tools for:

  • AI-generated social media content
  • Content scheduling and publishing
  • Channel and campaign management
  • Media upload automation
  • Performance tracking and insights
  • Integration with platforms such as YouTube and Facebook

The application uses Google OAuth authentication to allow users to securely authorize publishing actions on their own YouTube channels.
