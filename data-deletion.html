User Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: April 2026

At Insights Dash, users may request deletion of their personal data and connected account information at any time.

How to Request Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data, please send an email to:

contact@insightsdash.com

Please include:

  • Your full name
  • Your email address associated with the account
  • Your connected social media account information (if applicable)

Processing Time

We will review and process verified deletion requests within a reasonable timeframe.

What Will Be Deleted

This may include stored analytics data, connected account identifiers, scheduling data, and related account information.
